WHAT DO WE DO?

Traverous is a social travel journal app. It's a warm and welcoming community of travelers around the globe who share their travel experiences and inspire each other through animated travel stories created through traverous.

"We enable travelers to capture, revisit & discover travel experiences"

We’re now entering the era of mobile, but travel storytelling hasn’t evolved much further than longform travel blogs and general social media which are either cumbersome to create or not enough to explain the adventures of a traveler. They only tell emotions or state of a traveler at a particular place and fail to connect the dots. Our solution, on the contrary, captures those details in a really effortless manner and presents the chain of memories of the traveler in exact sequence he experienced it.
